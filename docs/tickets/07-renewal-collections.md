# Ticket: Renewal & Collections improvements

## Goal
Improve renewal forecasting and collections workflows with clearer risk insights and actions.

## Scope
- Renewal KPIs, charts, invoices and payments tables.
- Reminder and collection actions.

## Tasks
### Stream A — Insights & Metrics
- Add renewal risk scoring per account.
- Add overdue aging buckets and forecasted cash-in KPI.
- Add payment failure trend metric.

### Stream B — Visuals & UX
- Replace charts with renewal timeline and overdue bar chart.
- Add status badges and grouping by account.
- Add filters for overdue range.

### Stream C — Workflow & Actions
- Add “Send reminder” and “Start collection sequence” actions.
- Integrate alerts into Action Center queues.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Renewal risk and overdue KPIs are visible.
- Timeline and overdue bar charts replace placeholders.
- Invoices/payments have status badges and filters.
- Collection actions create Action Center items.
