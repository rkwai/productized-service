# Ticket: Delivery Reliability improvements

## Goal
Improve delivery predictability by adding risk insights, timeline visuals, and escalation workflows.

## Scope
- Delivery KPIs, charts, and milestones table.
- Sign-off, escalation, and reminder actions.

## Tasks
### Stream A — Insights & Metrics
- Add slippage risk prediction per milestone.
- Add owner reliability stats and historical trend.
- Add dependency risk flags.

### Stream B — Visuals & UX
- Replace charts with timeline/Gantt and slippage histogram.
- Add milestone status chips and group by workstream.
- Add “critical path” highlighting.

### Stream C — Workflow & Actions
- Add “Request sign-off” and “Escalate blocker” actions.
- Add calendar export and reminder scheduling.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Milestones show slippage risk and dependencies.
- Timeline and histogram replace placeholders.
- Critical path is visually highlighted.
- Actions support sign-off, escalation, and reminders.
