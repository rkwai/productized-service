# Ticket: Engagement Health improvements

## Goal
Surface drivers of engagement health, stronger visuals, and recovery workflows for low-health accounts.

## Scope
- Engagement KPIs, charts, and engagement table.
- Engagement recovery and escalation workflows.

## Tasks
### Stream A — Insights & Metrics
- Add engagement health driver breakdown (milestones, sentiment, risks).
- Add renewal forecast score per engagement.
- Add “scope creep” indicator based on change requests.

### Stream B — Visuals & UX
- Add health composition donut and trend line.
- Add engagement summary cards (owner, renewal, open risks).
- Highlight low-health engagements with status chips.

### Stream C — Workflow & Actions
- Add “Launch recovery playbook” action for low health.
- Route critical engagements to Action Center queues.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Engagement health drivers are visible and explainable.
- Charts provide composition and trend insights.
- Low-health engagements are flagged and actionable.
- Recovery actions create corresponding Action Center items.
